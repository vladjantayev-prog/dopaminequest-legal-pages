Privacy Policy

DopamineQuest

Last updated: April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DopamineQuest ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

1. Who We Are

DopamineQuest is operated by Vladislav Jantayev. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at dopaminequestapp@gmail.com.

2. Information We May Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may process the following information:

  • Text you enter into the app, such as micro-step responses or coach messages
  • Photos you submit for task or micro-step verification
  • Language preference and app settings
  • Subscription and purchase status
  • Basic usage and technical information needed for the app to function

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide AI coaching and task verification features
  • Show progress, streaks, levels, and completed tasks
  • Support subscriptions and paid features
  • Improve app stability, performance, and user experience
  • Respond to support requests

4. Screen Time / Family Controls

The app may use Apple Screen Time / Family Controls APIs to let you select distracting apps, enable protection for those apps, and temporarily restore access after completing eligible tasks.

This functionality is handled locally on your device unless otherwise stated in this Privacy Policy.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services to provide certain functionality. These may include:

  • OpenAI — for AI-generated coaching and verification responses
  • n8n — for workflow automation and request processing
  • RevenueCat — for subscription and purchase management
  • Apple — for In-App Purchases, subscriptions, and App Store services

These services may process data only as needed to provide app functionality.

6. Photos and Text Submitted by You

If you submit photos or text in the app, they may be processed in order to validate a task, validate a micro-step, or generate a coaching response.

Please avoid submitting sensitive personal information unless it is necessary for your use of the app.

7. Subscriptions and Payments

Payments and subscription handling are processed by Apple through In-App Purchases and may be managed through RevenueCat. We do not receive your full payment card details.

8. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the app, provide requested features, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

9. Your Choices

You may choose not to submit text or photos, but some app features may not work properly without them.

10. Children's Privacy

DopamineQuest is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date on this page.

12. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at dopaminequestapp@gmail.com.
